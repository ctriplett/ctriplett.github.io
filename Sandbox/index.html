Based in the Washington DC area, Dangerous Books specializes in the history, literature, and art of American social movements. We buy and sell books, pamphlets, broadsides, and other ephemera on radical politics, civil rights, labor history and organizing, agitprop, and just general rabble rousing.

Craig Triplett, bookseller.
